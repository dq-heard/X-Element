King Aaron

Starting with Call of Duty WWII as the first game I played seriously online to moving to Fortnite and having the epiphany to start an e-Sports team, X-Element is truly a display of the impact competitive gaming has had on my life.
With establishing an LLC and assembling a core team to get started, the realization came that running a professional operation is a full-time job. This led to me joining Full Sail University to pursue a Business Game & e-Sports Degree, which helps me to better support our members.
 My goal is to steer X-Element towards a more strategic approach in the e-Sports scene. Utilizing my creativity, vision, leadership, and guidance, I aim to lead the X-Element staff at all levels to achieve their fullest potential.

If you're interested in joining the team, add @xkingaaron on Discord and send your resume. Please be sure to include the game and position you're applying for within your message. We also encourage you to highlight your relevant skills, experience, and passion for gaming when applying.
