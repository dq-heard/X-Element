Current Season

X-Element is currently in off-season mode, which refers to the period of time in sports when playoff games have concluded and a leaguewide champion has been crowned. During this stage, players have a break from competition and focus on various activities such as rest, recovery, training, and pursuing personal endeavors.

Many players still participate in off-season leagues where they can showcase their abilities, stay in game shape, and compete against other professionals. These leagues often attract a mix of pro gamers and aspiring talent, creating a competitive environment that helps players stay sharp.

The off-season is also crucial for teams and management as they evaluate their performance from the previous year and make strategic decisions to improve their roster. This can involve adding new players through various avenues to strengthen the team for the upcoming campaign.
