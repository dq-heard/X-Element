What Is X-Element?

Mission and Identity

The X-Element logo symbolizes the universal challenge that every one of us faces in our lives. The 'X' serves as a metaphor for the core issue that each of us must confront: "How do I carve my niche in this landscape?" The 'E' represents us as individuals and the power we possess as a group by tying our love for gaming into our professional aspirations, likening those skills to distinct elements. When they unite, they collectively decode this riddle, paving the way to a respected and renowned presence in e-Sports.

Constructing a thriving, competitive team is a formidable challenge. Therefore, X-Element emphasizes a rigorous selection process, diligently searching for recruits distinguished by their exceptional skills, unwavering commitment, and sense of teamwork. Each member's distinct strengths collectively form the bedrock of what we need to succeed.

We also take pride in discovering untapped, overlooked talent and are committed to finding these individuals. We understand how people may go unseen due to various circumstances and X-Element aims to provide them with opportunities to shine. By offering a platform for these talents to showcase their skills, X-Element is not only striving to strengthen their own team, but also contribute to the overall growth and diversity of a new, cutting-edge industry.

Our Squads:

Our competitive players are represented by the element of Fire. These are the members who have earned the right to be on our team through forging their talents in the heat of online play.

Content creators and our other non-competitive streamers are represented by the element of Wind. They announce our team's presence to the world and spread word of our achievements within the e-Sports landscape.

Earth as an element represents our moderators, both on Discord and elsewhere. They help solidify our culture and ensure everyone has a positive experience while interacting with our channels.

The element of Water represents our friends and allies who see the vision and want to be a part of the community. Life can't be sustained without it and we make sure to appreciate our supporters.

At the core of our team is the Spirit, which represents our leadership and support staff. This includes our CEO, co-CEO, and other background members who help our players succeed or work behind the scenes for the brand.

Current Season

X-Element’s primary goal is to elevate our competitive performance. To achieve this, we plan to participate in LAN events, which can provide valuable experience and exposure to high-level competition. This can help the team improve their skills and strategies, leading to ultimately performing better in future competitions.

Another one of X-Element’s objectives is to establish our newly redesigned website as a professional platform for the team to share news, updates, and other content with our audience. In turn, we look forward to attracting more followers and building a stronger connection with our fanbase. Through our newly recruited social media squad, we look to bolster our online presence, creating a loyal and engaged community around X-Element.
